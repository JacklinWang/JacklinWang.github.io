A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Jacklin!

I'm a product designer at Apple on the World Wide Business Initiatives team designing an international consumer-facing service. I graduated from UC Davis Class of 2021 where I majored in Design and studied CS and business. I'm from Fremont, California, where I grew up spending school nights and weekends at family-owned restaurants or my dad's warehouse. From doodles on menus and placemats to sculptures from cardboard boxes, packaging tape, and zip-ties, I found passion in creativity. As I grew older, my art evolved with me and has sculpted my character and given me a unique way to interact with the world. In high school and college, design and art became a way to contribute to the world around me; painting murals for my community, designing graphics for my campuses and co-founding the first UX design club at my university. I am now seeking full-time opportunities in product design, where I can continue to influence people's lives by designing delightful experiences and using human-centered design to solve problems.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy other creative outlets such as getting my hands dirty with paint and clay for arts and crafts, taking photos, playing songs I love on the piano, or getting bruises from learning skateboard tricks. I love outdoor activities such as snowboarding, hiking, and beach volleyball.
